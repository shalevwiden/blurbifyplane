About Blurbify Plane

I designed this Blurbify plane to be the default flying image on my site, blurbify.
In order to have those circles and stuff on the plane I used mask css techniques and then used positioning and adjusted the top and right values for the circles and the text to get them into the right place. I also applied a lot of filters to the plane, for example huerotate() brightness() contrast() and saturate().

See the plane in motion at blurbifyapp.com
